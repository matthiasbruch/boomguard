BoomGuard
Modifications inspired by: Bonjwa + ReismitMais

Instructions

You know the game - you have to uncover 99 bombs.

Basic controls

  1. Left click: Uncover a field. It can go boom.
  2. Right click: Mark a field as safe. You only should do this if you're sure about it or you might get confused.
  3. Middle click: Uncovers all fields around the cursor... and yes ... all of them can go boom.

Actions

  1. Dig: Lets you uncover a field in a save way. It just costs time. Uncovering a mine (even when marked safe before) gives you [1 Credit].
  2. Scan: [2 Credits] Reveals what is behind covered fields. It reveals the selected field and those directly attached.
  3. Drop: [8 Credits] Carpet bombs a row of fields. Bombs on this line are deactivated and count as uncovered.
